About {{ title }}


{{ title }} is a software platform to manage active traffic cameras. It is developed by the group sw803f20 on Aalborg Univeritet as a student project.


Features

This webpage consistst of the main pages, reachable in the header.


Viewing live streams from traffic cameras

On the videoplayer page, you can watch live streams from all cameras that are active and you are given access to.


Recording video from traffic cameras

On the videoplayer page, you can choose a live stream and you can then record a video of a set length, with various options available. The video recorded will be available for download for a limited time on the download page after the recording has completed.

You can only have one recording active at a time per camera.


Setting up new cameras

As a user with rights to set up new cameras, this can be done on the setup camera page.

You must give some information about the camera and you can give permission to view the video feed from the camera to individual users.


Adjusting global settings

As administrator, there are multiple variables that can be tweaked on the settings page
